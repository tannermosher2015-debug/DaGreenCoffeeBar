The garden

You won't find us from the road.

Walk past the food trucks at South Maui Gardens and the heat drops away. Orchids climb the palms, a koi pond catches the light, and a monarch butterfly tree drifts overhead. That's where the bar is — open-air, in the shade.

We roast our beans in house every day and build our toast on sourdough from Blue Door Bakery next door. Order a macnut latte, find a nook, and stay a while. Most afternoons there's a local musician playing.

Suspended coffees

Buy one forward. We keep it on the board.

Pay for an extra coffee and we'll chalk it up. When someone who needs one comes by, it's already paid for — no questions. It's a small Kihei tradition we're proud to keep.

Ask the barista to add a suspended coffee to any order.
